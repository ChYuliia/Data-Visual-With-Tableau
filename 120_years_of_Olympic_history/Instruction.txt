In this module, you will take that data set and create your own story about the Olympic history.

Here's how to complete this assignment: 

    View the Getting Started with Kaggle (2:11) video. 
    Create an account and log in to Kaggle.
    Download the two files for Olympic data: 
        120 years of Olympic history: athletes and results
    Use Prep Builder to transform the data (if needed).
    Create a story based on the Olympics dataset.  For example - the United States, Russia, and China rivalry in Gymnastics during the Summer Olympics; Alpine Skiing during the Winter Olympics.  You can create your own story based on the data.
    Create appropriate visualizations (minimum 6) to support your story.
    Create Dashboards (minimum 3) based on the Tableau Sheets to support your story. 
    Create your own story using the Tableau story. (ex. title/annotations/highlighting data of interest)
    Save the Tableau file as Tableau Package Worksheet (.twbx) with data.
