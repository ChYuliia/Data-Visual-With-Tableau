Process

       1 Login to Data.World and get to the dataset. (https://data.world/nps/yellowstone-np)

       2 Download files for Yellowstone visitors.

       3 Review the glossary of the data. (https://data.world/nps/glossary)

       4 Optional: Check out the various source data. (https://irma.nps.gov/Stats/)

       5 Build a dashboard in Tableau (combine data sources if you want).

       6 Create at least 3 visualizations, using previous learning, focusing on the principles of color theory (new learning).

                Avoid red/green color palette.
                Use complementary colors - see the color wheel

       7 Upload the dashboard to assignment, Saved to PDF.
